Installation Guide

Android
iOS
  • Click the Android download button above to open Google Play.
  • Tap "Install" to begin downloading Forza Horizon 5 Mobile.
  • Once installation is complete, open the game from your app drawer.
  • Download additional resources if prompted in-game.
  • Login or create a new account to start playing!
  • Click the iOS download button above to open the App Store.
  • Tap "Get" then confirm installation with your Apple ID or Face ID.
  • After installation, launch Forza Horizon 5 Mobile from your home screen.
  • Allow any additional downloads in-game.
  • Login or create a new account and enjoy the game!
